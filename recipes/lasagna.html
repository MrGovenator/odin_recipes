Ingredients
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 28 ounce jar spaghetti sauce
  • 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 1 diced onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • salt to taste
  • pepper
Steps
  1. Heat a large pan with oil and cook meat until browned
  2. Remove meat and set to side. Add veg to pan and cook until soft
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrent.
